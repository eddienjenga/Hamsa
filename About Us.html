Hamsa is a company with it's main store in Nairobi Kenya. It mainly deals with the sale of sports products mainly shoes but other sports products can be found at our stores.
The first store was founded by Hamsa Chester whom the company is named after and who is the CEO and owner of all the Hamsa stores.
Hamsa has 20 stores in Kenya and 70 others spread across the world's major cities. Japan has 8 of our stores which is the highest number of stores in all countries outside of Africa.
The company has 9,500 employs , with 9,000 working in the stores while 500 work in delivery.
In the last financial year 2017-2018 our total net profit in sales was valued at $350,000,000,000 making us the leading sports store in the world.

The Hamsa Campaign has been ongoing for a month now.
It's a global campaign that requires people who are willing to donate their sports gears that
are still in good shape to the youth of their countries who are lacking and are active participants of sports. The Hamsa campaign involves the company's employees going in different regions all over the world to donate sports gears and helping the needy.
It's main aim is using sports to unite the world and also to promote sports.
